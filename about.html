About Us

We are the original Call of Duty stats team, founded on the CoD Competitive Reddit page. We grew extremely quickly and realized people really enjoyed what we were doing.

We made our Twitter and within a couple of months had around 7k followers. We then decided to partner with a Call of Duty coverage company however some internal issues meant we had to leave, leaving our Twitter behind unfortunately. We started from scratch and we are growing rapidly once again! Here on our website you will find stats exclusive to us these include standard deviation, in depth SND stats, and more! Here you can find stats for all of the major LAN events for COD Ghosts, and you will soon find Advance Warfare stats when the season starts. In the meantime you can check our twitter @CoD_Stats if you would like to see our tweets on the Advance Warfare online 2k, 5k, and 10k tournaments. We are the home of the most in-depth Call of Duty stats and we hope you enjoy your stay.
